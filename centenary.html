CELEBRATE THE CENTENARY WITH US

An avant-garde bat, an iconic image that was created to unite the past, present and future of Valencia CF on the occasion of the Centenary (1919-2019-âˆž)

It could not be other than a bat. Valencia CF makes public today, on the occasion of the Centenary season, a new identifying icon of the club, which goes back to its very roots with its most representative symbol and with an avant-garde design that looks to the future. Same club, new era.

At the gates of the 2018-19 season, the club further reinforces its identification with the bat, as an image to which it has been closely linked since its very foundation, almost a century ago, and which is linked to one of the institutional symbols of Valencia. Since the Middle Ages, the silhouette of the 'rat penat' with open wings has been a protagonist in the city's heraldry and is linked to the 'Real Senyera' itself.
